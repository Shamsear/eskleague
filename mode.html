KINGS CUP

  • Elite Division Cup.
  • Random 7 Matches will be played & then Semi and Final.
  • Rewards will be distributed for Knockout Golden Boot and Golden Glove.
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary included).

SUPER CUP

  • Division Cup.
  • Random 5 Matches will be played & then Semi and Final.
  • Rewards will be distributed for Knockout Golden Boot and Golden Glove.
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary included).

SUPER LEAGUE

  • Division League.
  • There will be 2 matches for each team from every Division.
  • Rewards will be distributed for Knockout Golden Boot and Golden Glove.
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary included).

PRO LEAGUE

  • 11 Players can selected from the squad to be used in this tournament.
  • One player from the selected squad will be removed after each loss.
  • You can add substitute player to Super Sunday List.
  • If anyone wins 4 games in a streak, they can retrieve one player which were removed.
  • Out of 11 players in the list, 6 prime cards can be used.
  • You can use 3 star or below players in the positions where players are not available or removed(These players should not have contract with other players).
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary not included).

MASTER LEAGUE

  • League will be split into 2 groups.
  • Group A players will have matches with Group B players and players in the in bottom of Group A.
  • After every round(2 Matches), certain no of teams will be eliminated from the league.
  • R2G ultimate squad can be used.
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary not included).

EURO CUP

  • You can choose any National team from Europe.
  • 5☆and 4☆ Players playing in choosen Nationality from the squad can only be used. Also players playing in selected Nation's League can also be used(Club logo is must).
  • 3☆,2☆ and 1☆ Players from the choosen National team can be used(Base only).
  • Group Round Matches
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary not included).

WORLD CUP

  • You can choose any National Team.
  • 5☆and 4☆ Players of choosen Nationality from the squad can only be used.
  • Also you can purchase 5☆and 4☆ Players from Efootball Store(CLub Coins will be charged).
  • 3☆,2☆ and 1☆ Players from the choosen National team can be used(Base only).
  • Group Round Matches
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary not included).

CLUB WORLD CUP

  • You can choose any Club.

  • 5☆and 4☆ Players playing in choosen Club from the squad can only be used.
  • 3☆,2☆ and 1☆ Players from the choosen club can be used.
  • Group Round Matches
  • 6 min match, Extra Time and Penalty off (salary not included).
